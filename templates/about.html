About Helpdesk Ticketing System

An ticket tracking system (also ITS, trouble ticket system, support ticket, request management or incident ticket system) is a computer software package that manages and maintains lists of tickets, as needed by an organization. ticket tracking systems are commonly used in an organization's customer support call center to create, update, and resolve reported customer tickets, or even tickets reported by that organization's other employees. A support ticket should include vital information for the account involved and the ticket encountered.An ticket tracking system often also contains a knowledge base containing information on each customer, resolutions to common problems, and other such data. An ticket tracking system is similar to a "bugtracker", and often, a software company will sell both, and some bugtrackers are capable of being used as an ticket tracking system, and vice versa. Consistent use of an ticket or bug tracking system is considered one of the "hallmarks of a good software team".
